Smart charger with edge inteligence, to promote green energy!

Adoption of electric vehicles requires smart charging infrastructure which can take decision concerning to the load on the grid, to shave off the peak load curves on the grids, by adapting the charging patterns. Smart charging allows a certain level of control over the charging process. It includes different pricing and technical charging options. The simplest form of incentive â€“ time-of-use pricing encourages consumers to move their charging from peak to off-peak, smart charging enables EVs to provide flexibility to grid

Smart charger proposed in this project is with advanced control mechanisms built with IoT integration ,can perform following actions

  • Remote switching ON and OFF of the charging
  • Unidirectional Control
  • Scheduled charging.
  • Energy Consumption Data logging.
  • Charging with solar power
  • Sophisticated bidirectional vehicle-to-home (V2H) mode.

In (V2H) mode EVs (Two Wheelers) are used as a residential back-up power supply during periods of power outage or to increase the self-consumption of energy produced on-site with the help of solar PV arrays. This significantly reduces the load on the grid as well as the vehicle owners get benefits from due to behind-the-meter optimisation and back-up power, this includes increasing self-consumption of locally produced renewable (solar) electricity as well as lowering dependence on the electricity grid and reducing the energy bill by buying low-cost electricity from the grid at off-peak hours and using it to supply homes using EVs to supply homes when the electricity tariff is higher.
